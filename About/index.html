Welcome to TLAS Electric Pty Ltd

Connecting Australia and East Africa through trusted, high-quality electronics.

Our Mission

At TLAS Electric Pty Ltd, we bridge the gap between Australia and East Africa by sourcing, restoring, and delivering reliable used electronics in bulk. Our mission is clear: to extend the lifespan of valuable electronics, minimize environmental impact, and cater to the increasing demand for affordable technology in East Africa's rapidly growing markets.

We believe in building trust and forging lasting partnerships. By collaborating closely with both suppliers and customers, we strive to exceed expectations. Our streamlined logistics ensure that international trade is seamless, efficient, and tailored to meet the needs of our partners.

At TLAS, we’re more than just a company connecting continents—we’re breaking barriers and shaping a brighter future for all. We believe that teamwork makes the dream work, and together, we’re creating lasting impacts and driving progress for communities in both Australia and East Africa.
