About Us

At TLAS ELECTRIC PTY LTD, we are passionate about bridging the gap between the Australian electronics market and the growing demand in Tanzania. By sourcing, refurbishing, and exporting quality electronics, we aim to contribute to the economic growth and technological advancement in Tanzania.

Our expert team is dedicated to ensuring the highest standards in product quality, while our innovative approach helps us stay ahead of the market trends. With a vision of enhancing local industries in Tanzania through access to the latest electronic solutions, we are building strong, sustainable partnerships for a prosperous future.
